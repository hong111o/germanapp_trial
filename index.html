Grammar Tips 📚
The basics, made simple
🎯 Der, Die, Das Articles
German has 3 genders: masculine (der), feminine (die), and neuter (das). Always learn the article with every new word!
der Mann · die Frau · das Kind
the man · the woman · the child
📝 Plurals Nouns
ALL plural nouns use die, no matter the original gender! Plural forms vary — learn each one with the word.
das Buch → die Bücher
the book → the books
🔤 V2 Word Order Sentences
The verb always comes second in a German sentence. Even if something else comes first, the verb stays in position 2.
Heute lerne ich Deutsch.
Today I learn German. (verb = 2nd)
🌟 Umlauts ä ö ü Pronunciation
Umlauts are modified vowels. ä = "air", ö = "er" with round lips, ü = "ee" with round lips. The ß = sharp "ss".
schön · Mädchen · über · Straße
beautiful · girl · over · street
🔢 Numbers 1–10 Basics
Learn numbers early — they're used constantly!
1 ein · 2 zwei · 3 drei · 4 vier · 5 fünf
6 sechs · 7 sieben · 8 acht · 9 neun · 10 zehn
💡 Negation Sentences
Use nicht to negate verbs/adjectives, and kein/keine to negate nouns (like "no" + noun).
Ich trinke nicht. / Ich habe keine Zeit.
I don't drink. / I have no time.
